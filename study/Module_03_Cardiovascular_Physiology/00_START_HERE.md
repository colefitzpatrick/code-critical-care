## Why this module (your unfair advantage)
This is **fluid mechanics**: pressure = flow × resistance. You already own the math —
we're just relabeling pipes as vessels and a pump as the heart. Everything in
cardiovascular **patho** (Module 04), shock, and CRNA hemodynamics hangs off these
equations.

## The two equations that run the whole module
```
   CO  = HR × SV          (cardiac output)
   MAP = CO × SVR          (Ohm's law for the body)
```
Master these and you can predict what any drug, bleed, or septic patient does to a BP.

## What "mastered" looks like
Given a change (↓volume, vasodilation, fast AF, a vasodilator drug), you can walk
CO/SV/SVR/MAP step by step and say the nurse's response — from a blank page.
