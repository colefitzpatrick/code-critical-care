This is the **integrator module** — the kidney controls almost everything you've learned.

## Why this one matters most
The kidney is the master regulator of **K⁺ (Mod 1/2), acid–base (Mod 2), volume & Na⁺
(Mod 2), and blood pressure via RAAS (Mod 3/4).** When it fails (AKI), all of those break
at once → that's the AEIOU you met in Module 2, and why CRRT exists. Owning this module
makes half of critical care click into place.

## What "mastered" looks like
You can classify any AKI as pre-renal / intrinsic / post-renal, predict its labs
(K⁺, HCO₃⁻, BUN:Cr), name the dialysis indication (AEIOU), and protect a fistula —
from a blank page.
