# Module 08 — Neuro: ICP, Stroke, Seizures · START HERE 🔓

⏱️ ~6 sessions. Science + **amplified nursing** (`07_NCLEX_and_Nursing_Actions.md`).

## Why
The brain has **no fuel reserve** — it lives second-to-second on blood flow (glucose + O₂). That makes it the most ischemia-vulnerable organ and turns everything you learned about perfusion, Na⁺, and CO₂ into life-or-death neuro nursing. ICP, stroke, and seizures are core ICU/CCRN and heavily NCLEX-tested.

## The one equation that runs the module
**CPP = MAP − ICP** (cerebral perfusion pressure = inflow pressure minus back-pressure). Keep it ≥ ~60.

## What "mastered" looks like
You can explain Monro-Kellie + CPP, recognize rising ICP (incl. Cushing's triad), run an ischemic-vs-hemorrhagic stroke decision, and manage a seizure — from a blank page.
