## Why
Blood = transport + defense + clotting; the immune system is the body's army; **sepsis** is the host–pathogen battle gone systemic (Mod 01). This module ties together transfusions, coagulation, **DIC** (the runaway clotting from Mod 01 Ch7), neutropenia, and the deep dive on sepsis/septic shock — all core ICU/CCRN.

## What "mastered" looks like
You can work up anemia, run a transfusion safely (and catch a reaction), explain coagulation + DIC, apply neutropenic precautions, and run the sepsis Hour-1 bundle — from a blank page.
