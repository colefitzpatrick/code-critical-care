## TIER 1
```
                 PUMP FAILS / ARTERY BLOCKS / RHYTHM FAILS
                                  │
                                  ▼
                          ↓ Cardiac Output
                                  │
                 ┌────────────────┼─────────────────┐
                 ▼                 ▼                  ▼
        BAROREFLEX (fast)   RAAS (slow)        SNS (catecholamines)
        ↑HR, ↑SVR           ↑Na⁺/H₂O, ↑SVR     ↑HR, ↑contractility
                 └────────────────┼─────────────────┘
                                  ▼
                 ↑ PRELOAD + ↑ AFTERLOAD on a weak pump
                                  ▼
                CONGESTION (lungs/body)  +  ↑ O₂ demand
                                  ▼
                        worse pump  ──►  spiral (Mod 01 Ch7)

  LEFT HF  → pulmonary congestion: dyspnea, crackles, ↓O₂, orthopnea
  RIGHT HF → systemic congestion: leg/sacral edema, JVD, weight↑, liver/abd
  MI       → chest pain, ↑troponin, ECG changes → reperfuse FAST
  AF       → irregular, stroke risk (clot), poor filling
  TREATMENT = reverse the compensation: ↓preload ↓afterload ±↑contractility, O₂, reperfuse
```
