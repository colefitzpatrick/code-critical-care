## Why
Endocrine = **hormone feedback loops** — the control-systems chapter (your engineering wheelhouse, Mod 01 Ch6). Each gland has a set point; disease = too much or too little hormone. Diabetes/DKA, thyroid storm, adrenal crisis, and SIADH/DI are core ICU/CCRN + heavily NCLEX-tested, and they tie straight into your acid-base (Mod 2) and Na⁺ (Mod 7/8) work.

## What "mastered" looks like
