# Module 01 — Homeostasis & the Cell · START HERE

⏱️ Whole module ≈ 4–6 short sessions. **Never more than one numbered file per sitting.**

## Why this module is first (the "why")
Almost every disease you will ever nurse is one of two failures:
1. **A gradient broke** (something that should be separated mixed, or a pump failed), or
2. **A feedback loop broke** (a control system lost its set point or its sensor/effector).

If you own this module, the rest of pathophysiology stops being memorization and becomes
**"which control system or gradient failed, and what does that predict at the bedside?"**
That is clinical reasoning. That is the whole game.

## What "mastered" looks like here
You can stand at a whiteboard and, from blank, draw how a cell keeps its insides
different from its outside, explain *why* that matters when a patient's potassium is
6.8, and say what you'd assess — without notes.
