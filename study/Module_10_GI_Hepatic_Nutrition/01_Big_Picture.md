# 01 · The Big Picture

## One-sentence model
> **The GI tract digests/absorbs fuel and is a barrier; the liver is the chemical plant that detoxifies, synthesizes, and stores — lose the plant and the whole body's chemistry destabilizes.**

## The liver's jobs (what fails in liver disease)
| Job | Fails → |
|---|---|
| **Detox** (ammonia → urea, drugs, toxins) | ↑ammonia → **hepatic encephalopathy**; drug accumulation |
| **Synthesis** (albumin, clotting factors) | low albumin → **ascites/edema**; **coagulopathy/bleeding** (↑INR) |
| **Bile** production | jaundice, fat malabsorption |
| **Glucose storage** (glycogen) | hypoglycemia |
| **Filtering portal blood** | **portal hypertension** → **varices** + ascites + splenomegaly |

## Engineering translation
- Liver = a refinery + detox plant + warehouse on the portal "inflow pipe."
- Cirrhosis = the refinery scarred/clogged → back-pressure (**portal hypertension**) blows out collateral vessels (**varices** that hemorrhage) and leaks fluid (**ascites**).
- No clotting-factor synthesis → the patient bleeds (and a varix is a high-pressure bleed in a patient who can't clot — a deadly combo).

## The big GI emergencies
- **GI bleed** — upper (varices, peptic ulcer) vs lower; → hypovolemic shock (Mod 3).
- **Pancreatitis** — pancreatic enzymes autodigest the pancreas (↑lipase/amylase).
- **Bowel obstruction / perforation** — surgical; third-spacing, sepsis risk.
- **Refeeding syndrome** — feeding a starved patient → insulin surge → **↓PO₄/↓K⁺/↓Mg²⁺** (Mod 2).

## 5 anchors
1. Liver = **detox + synthesis + bile + storage + portal filter.**
2. Cirrhosis → **portal hypertension → varices (bleed) + ascites + encephalopathy + coagulopathy.**
3. **GI bleed = hypovolemic shock** until proven otherwise → ABCs, fluids/blood, find source.
4. **Hepatic encephalopathy = ammonia** → lactulose to dump it.
5. **Refeeding syndrome:** start feeds **slow**, replace PO₄/K⁺/Mg²⁺.
