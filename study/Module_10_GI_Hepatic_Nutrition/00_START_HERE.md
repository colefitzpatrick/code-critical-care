## Why
The gut absorbs and the **liver is the body's chemical plant** — when it fails, clotting, ammonia, albumin, and drug metabolism all break at once. GI bleeds and pancreatitis are common ICU admissions; refeeding ties back to your electrolytes (Mod 2).

## What "mastered" looks like
You can list the liver's jobs and predict what fails in cirrhosis, manage a GI bleed, recognize pancreatitis and refeeding syndrome — from a blank page.
