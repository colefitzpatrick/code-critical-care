## Why this module (the link back to 01)
Module 01 gave you the cell, gradients, osmosis, and feedback loops. Module 02 is those
exact ideas running the **whole body's water and pH**:
- Fluid shifts = osmosis/tonicity at body scale (Module 01 Chain 5).
- Electrolytes = the Module 01 gradients, now as **labs you act on**.
- Acid–base = **buffer chemistry + two control loops** (lungs fast, kidneys slow). This
  is your single biggest engineering edge — it's a log buffer equation and a control
  system. Most nursing students fear ABGs. You will not.

## What "mastered" looks like
You can interpret an arterial blood gas (ABG) cold with a 5-step algorithm, explain
*why* vomiting causes alkalosis, predict the K⁺ when the pH moves, and state the nurse's
action for each derangement — from a blank page.
